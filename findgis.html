Vector Data Formats

  • Shapefile (.shp) open
    • Pro Tip: a shapefile is more than one file!
  • GeoDatabase (.gdb) proprietary
  • GeoJson (.geojson) open, good for web
  • KML/KMZ (.kml/kmz) open, Google vector format
  • GeoPackage (.gpkg) open

    • Raster Data Formats

  • GeoTIFF (.tif) open
  • Esri grid proprietary
  • ASCII (.asc) open, but not easy
  • BIL (.bil) open
  • NetCDF (.nc) open, usually climate data
  • Many more!
